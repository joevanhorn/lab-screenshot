# Configure MFA Policy

## Step 1: Navigate to the policy
1. Navigate to the Authentication Policies page
2. Click on the "Employee Access" rule's Actions dropdown
3. Click Edit

[SCREENSHOT: The Employee Access rule's Edit dialog showing the policy configuration]

## Step 2: Change authentication settings
1. In the Edit Rule dialog, scroll down to the THEN section
2. Change "User must authenticate with" from "Password" to "Password + Another factor"
3. Click Save
4. Click "Save anyway" in the confirmation dialog

[SCREENSHOT: The policy rule showing Password + Another factor after saving]
